# 🐾 BitBuddy

A virtual pet that lives in your terminal. Feed it, play with it, and keep it alive — or don't. No judgment.

## Features
- 🍕 Feed your buddy to keep hunger down
- 🎾 Play with your buddy to boost happiness
- 😴 Let your buddy rest to restore energy
- 📊 Visual stat bars for hunger, happiness and energy
- 💾 Auto-saves between sessions so your buddy persists
- 💀 Neglect your buddy long enough and... well...

## How to use
1. Clone this repo
2. Run: `python bitbuddy.py`
3. Name your buddy and keep them alive!
